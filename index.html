I.C. Stars Project

    During my I.C. Stars internship, sponsored by United Airlines, 
    we tackled a project aiming to collect and streamline data on 
    the airline's equipment. Using Angular as our platform, we formed 
    teams to propose a innovative solution for United that we will 
    work on for entire internship. I leveraged my technical background 
    to guide the team and conceptualize the app.

    Our solution involved United airport workers conducting equipment 
    walkthroughs, guided by an embedded checklist in the app. Using their 
    phone's camera, workers could snap pictures, and the app's computer vision 
    recognized equipment, overlaying "pin-shaped" filters. Worker-assigned colors 
    (green, yellow, red) indicated equipment health. The images, along with additional 
    information, were sent to an analyst's dashboard, managing service tickets and 
    groupings. The app, featuring augmented reality with Unity and Vulfora, 
    allowed workers to visualize the pins placed by others using thier camera phones, 
    ensuring walkthrough integrity.
    
    United Airlines selected our solution, naming us winners.
